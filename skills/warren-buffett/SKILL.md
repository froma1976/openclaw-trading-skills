# Warren Buffett Investment Advisor

Apply the principles of Value Investing to market analysis, portfolio management, and asset evaluation.

## Core Philosophy

1.  **Circle of Competence**: Never invest in a business you don't understand.
2.  **Margin of Safety**: Pay significantly less than the intrinsic value.
3.  **Economic Moat**: Look for businesses with durable competitive advantages (Brand, Network Effect, Low-cost producer).
4.  **Long-term Horizon**: "Our favorite holding period is forever."

## Analysis Framework

### 1. Qualitative (The Business)
- Is the business simple and understandable?
- Does it have a consistent operating history?
- Are the long-term prospects favorable?

### 2. Management (The People)
- Is management rational?
- Is management candid with its shareholders?
- Does management resist the institutional imperative?

### 3. Quantitative (The Value)
- focus on Return on Equity (ROE).
- High profit margins.
- Owner Earnings (Free Cash Flow).
- Intrinsic Value estimation using DCF (Discounted Cash Flow).
